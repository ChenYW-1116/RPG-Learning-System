【診斷摘要】
整體表現穩定，Band 7.5-8.5 區間，但第 4 次驟降至 6.5，顯示流程圖或步驟描述仍可能出現「一次性失誤」。LR: Process Verbs 雖然分數 0.81，但因其 IELTS 權重高達 19 %，成為最大扣分缺口；CC: Referencing 與 TA: Logic Accuracy 次之。

【關鍵瓶頸分析】
1. Process Verbs 深度不足：過度依賴 “start → then → finally”，缺少「循環、回流、副產物」等進階動詞（recirculate, discharge, condense）。
2. Referencing 模糊：this / these 指代時，先行詞與圖中階段數字未精準對應，造成閱卷人追�負擔。
3. Logic Accuracy 波動：第 4 次寫作可能在「並行步驟」或「材料分支」漏掉關鍵箭頭，導致敘述跳躍。

【具體改進建議】
A. 建立「流程動詞庫」：針對加熱、冷卻、分離、混合四大語義場，各記 5 個高頻動詞＋介副詞搭配（e.g., the mixture is heated *indirectly* via a steam jacket）。  
B. 強制「雙標」制度：每段首句先給階段編號（S1, S2…），再於細節句使用 “At this stage, …” 明確回指。  
C. 完稿「逆向校對」：用 30 秒只檢查箭頭→句子，看是否每條線都有主詞＋動詞＋受詞，杜絕遺漏。

[Diagnostic Summary]
Overall performance hovers around Band 7.5–8.5, yet the drop to 6.5 on attempt 4 signals a “one-off” lapse in process-description logic. LR: Process Verbs, despite a 0.81 score, carries the heaviest IELTS weight (≈19 %) and remains the biggest drag; CC: Referencing and TA: Logic Accuracy follow.

[Key Bottleneck Analysis]
1. Shallow process-verb range: over-reli your go-to chain “starts → then → finally” lacks advanced verbs for loops, by-products or feedback flows (recirculate, discharge, condense).  
2. Slippery referencing: demonstratives (this / these) do not always lock back to numbered stages, forcing examiners to re-scan.  
3. Logic wobble: the 6.5 script probably skipped a parallel branch or return arrow, creating a narrative gap.

[Actionable Advice]
A. Build a “process-verb bank”: for each semantic field—heating, cooling, separating, mixing—list five verbs plus adverbial collocations (e.g., the mixture is heated *indirectly* via a steam jacket).  
B. Impose a “double-anchor” rule: open every paragraph with the stage label (S1, S2 …) and echo it with “At this stage, …” in the detail sentence.  
C. Reverse-proof: spend 30 s scanning arrows → sentences to confirm every line has a subject + verb + object, plugging omissions.
